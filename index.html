    Welcome to my website!

    As a data scientist with a rich background from UC Berkeley, my journey is driven by a profound commitment to using data for social good. Among the most recent projects I've led and contributed to is a detailed climate vulnerability assessment in the Central San Joaquin Valley, leveraging geospatial analysis to pinpoint vulnerable populations. This effort, alongside pioneering disaster response strategies through the simulation of alternative aviation methods and assessing airborne transmission risks within healthcare settings, highlights my dedication to public health and safety.

    Beyond these recent contributions, I am a passionate learner, constantly deepening my understanding of machine learning, data visualization, and data analysis. These interests not only fuel my professional endeavors but also guide my exploration of new ways to interpret and visualize data, making complex information accessible and actionable.
